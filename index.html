Who are we?

Gabriel M Isserlis

Chuck R Evans

Chuck and Gabriel have been working together for about a year, exploring the market and needs of their target audience, shaping their plans, and doing their best to build their first company.

While a lot of progress has been made, they decided it was time to find a resource that would help move everything forward at a significantly faster pace.

Gabriel applied and was accepted to Founder's Institute in London. In early 2018, he will be putting DBMA Ltd through its paces, evolving, growing, and by April, will either be leading an exciting new company, or will have crashed out of the program in spectacular fashion.

In either case, the future is exciting, and Gabriel will do his utmost to share his journey in newsletter and vlog forms, in the name of science!
